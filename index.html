Go: River & Mountain

A terrain-based variant of the ancient game of Go

Height Rule

To place on a mountain of height h, you need an adjacent friendly stone at height h or h−1. You cannot jump heights (e.g. 0 → 2 is still forbidden).

River cells block all placement and provide no liberties.
